Places to Go and Things to do in 23rd Avenue



Alphabet District in Portland is one of the fun areas to visit, whether you're a native here, adopted this place as your home, or visiting, this small area of downtown should be on your must-see places.



I used to work in one of the shops down at 23rd Ave, and would just walk around while on my lunch. There is no lack of places to eat, spots to sit on, and enjoy a sandwich, and definitely plenty of stores to check out, local or chain.

On my days off, I walk my dog here, starting from Petco's Unleashed, all the way down to All For Paws, and back again.
